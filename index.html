NORTH KOREA...

North Korea remains one of the world’s most repressive states. In his seventh year in power, Kim Jong-Un—who serves as chairman of the States Affairs Commission and head of the ruling Workers’ Party of Korea—continues to exercise almost total political control.

More than 24 million North Koreans live under tyranny. They are subjected to widespread human rights violations and denied fundamental rights like free expression, association, assembly, and religion.
