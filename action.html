HOW CAN YOU HELP?...

START A FUNDRAISING CAMPAIGN

North Koreans face some of the worst human rights abuses happening in the world today. Many risk their lives to escape their country. But when they cross into China, they’re still not free.

As a fundraiser, your efforts are not only raising vital funds for defectors, but you’re also changing the conversation about North Korea to focus on the people. If you have any questions or need more support, please contact NKDA's Assistance Center at +44 20 7227 5500 or email us at needyou@KNDKA.uk

BECOME A NKDA HOST

Hosting means more than providing a bed and meals. You’ll welcome a new member into your home, share your everyday lives, and grow as you get to know each other and share your cultural perspectives.We have placed thousands of people, for anything from a week to months and years and notched up hundreds of nights of shelter.
