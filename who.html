WHO WE ARE...

MISSION

We seek a world in which all defectors get to live with dignity and security outside North Korea.

VISION

NKDA provide safety, medical treatment, education and financial support to defectors in China and Russia.

North Korea Defectors Assistance was founded in 2017 by Seong Jae-sang, who managed to escape to South Korea after living in hiding for almost 10 years. At age 13, his mother was publicly executed and shortly after he was separated from his sister. Years later, he took the decision to move to the United Kingdom to become the founder of NKDA. We are a nonprofit organization that responds to the worst humanitarian crisis to date and helps all defectors gain control of their future.
